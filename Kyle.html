Kyle Rose, a.k.a. Swole is a dedicated Nutrition and Wellness Coach with a passion for guiding bodybuilding athletes and individuals on transformative lifestyle journeys. With a wealth of experience in both personal competition and coaching, Kyle brings a unique blend of expertise to his clients.

As an NPC Nationally Qualified Mens Physique and Classic Physique bodybuilder, Kyle boasts an impressive track record of multiple first-place finishes and overall placings in just four years . His journey extends into the classroom, holding a Bachelor of Science in Applied Kinesiology with minors in Psychology and Nutritional Sciences from Rutgers University. A seasoned NASM certified personal trainer with nearly a decade of experience, Kyle also holds specializations in Corrective Exercise (CES) and Certified Nutrition Coach (CNC). Currently working with predominantly high school and college athletes, Kyle has made a significant impact on the lives of hundreds over the years, enhancing their overall quality of life through coaching and training.

For Kyle, life's fulfillment lies in empowering others to become the best versions of themselves. His unwavering passion and purpose revolve around helping individuals achieve their dreams, fostering a community built on knowledge, love, and personal growth. Kyles priorities are a healthy mind, healthy body and healthy soul. Continual growth and transformation is not just a goal; it's a way of life.
